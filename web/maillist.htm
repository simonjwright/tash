The TASH mailing list is provided courtesy of Calspan, the Buffalo, NY, operation of Veridian Engineering, and is intended for discussion of TASH usage and development. You can subscribe to the TASH mailing list by sending email to listserv@calspan.com with the following command as the only thing in the body of your message:

      subscribe tash John Doe

Of course, you'd replace "John Doe" with your own name!

When you subscribe, the LISTSERV software will send you an email confirming your subscription and providing instructions on how to post messages, get help, and unsubscribe.
